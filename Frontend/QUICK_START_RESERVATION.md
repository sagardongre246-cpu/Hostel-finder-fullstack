# Quick Start - Reservation Modal

## 🚀 What's New

A fully functional reservation modal with integrated price comparison is now live on your website!

## 📍 Where to Find It

1. Navigate to any hostel details page
2. Look at the **right sidebar**
3. Find the blue button: **"Reserve Your PG Stay"**
4. Click it!

## ✨ What You'll See

### The Modal Opens With:
- Hostel name and price at the top
- A comprehensive booking form
- A "We Price Match" section
- A blue "Complete Reservation" button

### Form Fields:
- **Full Name** - Your name
- **Email** - Your email address
- **Phone** - Your contact number
- **Check-in Date** - When you arrive
- **Check-out Date** - When you leave
- **Guests** - How many people (1-6)
- **Rooms** - How many rooms (1-4)
- **Special Requests** - Any special needs (optional)

### Price Match Feature:
- Click the yellow **"We Price Match"** button
- Watch it compare prices from 6 platforms
- See the best price highlighted in green
- View how much you save

## 🎯 How to Use

### Step 1: Open Modal
Click "Reserve Your PG Stay" button

### Step 2: Fill Form
Enter your details in all required fields (marked with *)

### Step 3: Select Dates
Use the date pickers for check-in and check-out

### Step 4: Choose Guests/Rooms
Select from the dropdown menus

### Step 5: (Optional) Check Prices
Click "We Price Match" to see price comparison

### Step 6: Submit
Click "Complete Reservation" to finish

## 💡 Tips

- **Required fields** are marked with an asterisk (*)
- **Date validation** ensures check-out is after check-in
- **Price match** takes 1.5 seconds to load (simulated)
- **Close modal** by clicking X or clicking outside
- **Form resets** when you close and reopen
